Personal projects, treated as one more org. Unlike a company org, nesting here is flexible — derive levels from what actually exists (see the `claudemd-author` skill):

- A product with several repos → `private/<Product>/CLAUDE.md` (product level) + repo-level files inside each repo.
- A single-repo mono-project → just its own repo-level `CLAUDE.md` with a short what/why folded in; no product layer.
